ABOUT

28

Shop

A Japan-based online shop established on July 2020, 28 Shop specializes in reselling jewelries from prominent brands and auction pieces through live-streams. Offering interactive shopping experience and delightful customer service, we guarantee seamless purchasing process provided with real-time personal recommendations.

Our shop offers an exclusive selection of watches, rings, necklaces, bracelets, and other accessories from well-known luxury brands, ensuring authentic and high-quality products.
